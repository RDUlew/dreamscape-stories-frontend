Our Vision

Did you know The average person spends six years of their life dreaming?

We all have had dreams that we thought about for days with no idea what it meant for our life. Our Goal was to create an opportunity for you to share those dreams! We believe you build community by relating to other people. We wanted to open up a space for you to build community with others that are dreaming the same thing.
